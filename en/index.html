Three pillars of our expertise

We remove the barriers that hold back your investments

Our work doesn't end with the project documentation. We take over negotiations with the distributor, landowners and the relevant authorities and drive them through to a legally valid permit.

For PV and BESS developers

Grid connection & approvals

Connecting a large-capacity source or battery storage system to the grid typically gets delayed at the distributor. We guide you through the entire process — from the connectability study and RTU communication setup with the dispatch centre, to obtaining the final UPOS (trial operation for compliance verification) and UTP (permit for permanent operation) for systems above 100 kW. All in coordination with ČEZ Distribuce, EG.D or PRE.

For linear and transport structures

MV/LV line relocation engineering

A clash between the route and the distribution network stops the entire construction process. We secure the design and the relocation permit, handle the property-law agenda, easements (SoBS VB — future easement agreements) and temporary traffic measures (DIO), and represent you at site inspection days — so construction continues on schedule and without contractual penalties.

For commercial and industrial sites

Securing MV capacity

A site needs a higher reserved capacity that isn't freely available from the distributor. We design the power supply solution — including battery integration for peak shaving and smart substations (smart grids) — negotiate the reserved capacity and optimise your additional connection costs.

FAQ

What investors ask most often

Our service covers the entire life cycle of a relocation: from securing the initial statement on the existence of networks, through the technical design of the new routing, to arranging temporary traffic measures (DIO) and coordinating with the distribution system operator (DSO). The key added value is our administrative back office, which processes the property-law agenda in bulk and within statutory deadlines, including the establishment of future easement agreements (SoBS VB) with the affected landowners — eliminating the risk of construction delays.
Connecting generation and storage above 100 kW is subject to the strict Distribution System Operating Rules (PPDS) and the Lex OZE III legislation. As experts holding the IT00 authorisation, we take full responsibility for the process with the system operators (ČEZ, EG.D, PRE). We design compliant single-line diagrams and ensure the technical requirements are met, including integration of the remote terminal unit (RTU). We then safely guide the client through the UPOS phase (trial operation for compliance verification) all the way to the final UTP (permit for permanent operation).
If the local distributor rejects a request to increase capacity because the transformer is exhausted, we carry out a technical-regulatory audit of the connection point. Rather than building expensive new lines, we focus on integrating Smart Grid technologies and installing compact switchgear (e.g. ABB SafeRing). We design technical solutions that limit unwanted reverse flows into the grid and implement peak-shaving systems using battery buffers. Based on this data, we negotiate entirely new, economically optimal connection terms with the distributor.
In large corporate design offices, your project often ends up with less experienced juniors and becomes just an item in a long schedule. In our engineering boutique we keep a strict limit on the number of projects handled simultaneously. Every technical solution, calculation and drawing is personally prepared and guaranteed by an authorised engineer in the field of Technological Equipment of Buildings (IT00), with a track record of hundreds of successfully defended investment projects. The client therefore communicates directly with the person who actually works on the project.
Our speed comes from a clear division of roles within the team and from many years of experience. Since 2018 we have acted as a direct supplier to ČEZ Distribuce, a.s., which means we know the internal methodologies, approval procedures and decision-making processes of the large distributors from the inside. We also have senior administrative support that fully absorbs the bureaucratic agenda. While the engineer handles the purely expert tasks, the back office works daily in distribution portals, at building authorities and in the land registry — shortening processes by as much as tens of percent.
